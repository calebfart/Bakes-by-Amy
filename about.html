About Amy's Bakes

Bakes by Amy was born out of North Carolina, founded by a passionate woman named Amy. With years of experience in the food industry, Amy started her journey at a local Food Lion. She began as a staff member in the deli and bakery department and, through hard work and dedication, gradually worked her way up the ranks to manager. Amy's commitment to excellence and love for baking were the driving forces behind her idea. In late 2024 and early 2025, after years of honing her skills and building her passion, she decided to take the leap and turn her dream into a reality. Bakes by Amy was established, offering a delicious variety of freshly baked treats, including cupcakes, cookies, cakes, and more. Amy's vision was to share her homemade, carefully crafted baked goods with the community, all made with love and the finest ingredients.
